More Info

A game developer is a skilled professional who designs, creates, and programs video games. They are responsible for almost every aspect of video game production, from the initial concept phase through to the final testing. Game development involves a wide range of skills and disciplines, including programming, design, art, audio, and quality assurance. Game developers may work on a variety of projects, from simple mobile games to complex console titles.
